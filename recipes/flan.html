Flan

Caramel flan dessert, a delightful finish to any meal

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • Evaporated milk
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cream cheese
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix the sugar with water and heat it up to create the caramel, place it at the bottom of the bowl
  2. Beat the eggs with the cream cheese, when the mix is uniform add the remaining ingredients
  3. Pour the mix over the caramel in the bowl
  4. Bake for 50-60 minutes at 350 degrees F
  5. Remove the recipient, let it cool for 1 hour and the refrigerate
  6. Enjoy!
