Buñuelos

A tradicional christmas treat

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of fresh farmer cheese
  • 3/4 cup of cornstarch
  • 1/4 yuca flour
  • 2 tablespoon of sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1/4 tablespoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients except oil in a bowl and mix them up
  2. Form small balls with the dough
  3. Drop the balls in warm oil for 3 to 4 minutes
  4. Remove from the oil and drain the excess oil with paper towels
  5. Enjoy!
