Shrimp tempura rolls

A delicios sushi roll made up with shrimp, avocado, rice, seaweed and more. The mix of flavors make this roll my absolute favorite!

Ingredients

  • Shrimp tempura
  • Avocado
  • Seaweed
  • Sushi rice
  • Seasoned rice vinegar
  • Cucumber

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice under water
  2. Cook the rice for 10 minutes
  3. Season the rice
  4. Bake the shrimp
  5. Place the seaweed on a plastic wrapping and add the rice and shrimp on top
  6. Add the ingredients that are missing
  7. Use the sushi mat to form the sushi
  8. Enjoy!
